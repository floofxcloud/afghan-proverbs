The Proverb

آب دریا از دست مردم می‌رود

English Translation

The sea’s water is lost by the people.

Pashto Translation

د سمندر اوبه د خلکو له لاسه ځي

Meaning in Persian: این مثل بیان می‌کند که منابع بزرگ هم اگر به درستی استفاده نشوند، می‌توانند تمام شوند. حتی دریا با آن بزرگی‌اش اگر بی‌توجهی شود، آبش از بین می‌رود.

Meaning in English: This proverb emphasizes that even the greatest resources can be depleted if they are misused or not cared for. It’s a warning that carelessness and overuse—even by many—can exhaust even the sea.

Cultural Meaning: In Afghan culture, this proverb serves as a reminder to act responsibly with what we have, whether it be water, wealth, time, or relationships. It reflects communal responsibility and the importance of preserving what may seem infinite.
