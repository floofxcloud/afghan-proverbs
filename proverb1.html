The Proverb

هر چیزی که رنگ می‌بازد، با گذشت زمان باز می‌شود

English Translation

Everything that fades will regain its color with the passage of time.

Pashto Translation

هر څه چې رنګ ورکوي، د وخت په تېرېدو سره بیرته رنګ اخلي

Meaning in Persian: این مثل به این معناست که زمان همه چیز را بهبود می‌بخشد و مشکلات و سختی‌ها با گذشت زمان از بین می‌روند

Meaning in English: This proverb signifies that time heals all wounds. Problems and hardships fade over time, and everything that once seemed lost or faded will return to a better state.

Cultural Meaning: In Afghan culture, this proverb is often used to offer hope and encouragement. It reflects the belief that perseverance through tough times will lead to eventual peace and happiness.
