The Proverb

هر که درختی کاشت، درختی دیگر در دل خود کاشته است

English Translation

Whoever plants a tree has also planted another tree in their heart.

Pashto Translation

هر څوک چې ونه کښي، په زړه کې یې بله ونه کښل شوې ده

Meaning in Persian: این مثل بیان‌گر این است که کاشتن درخت یا انجام کارهای خوب، نه تنها به محیط اطراف کمک می‌کند بلکه باعث رشد درونی و آرامش در قلب انسان نیز می‌شود.

Meaning in English: This proverb expresses that doing good deeds—like planting a tree—not only benefits the world, but also nurtures the soul. Planting life around us is symbolic of planting goodness within ourselves.

Cultural Meaning: In Afghan culture, trees represent life, growth, and hope. This proverb reflects the belief that contributing to nature or the community brings personal fulfillment. It also encourages environmental responsibility and spiritual growth.
